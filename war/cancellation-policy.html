SalesMaxx Policy

Cancellation & Refunds Policy

All cancellations attract a 15% surcharge. Refunds are made (Subject to surcharge above),

  • • 75% of payment, minimum of 3 weeks to the booked date.
  • • 50% of payment, 2 weeks to the booked date.
  • • 0% of payment, 7 working days or less to the booked date.

Rescheduling & Transfer Policy

All rescheduled or transfers attracts a 5% surcharge. Rescheduling and transfers are made (subject to the above surcharge) and the following below:

  • • Based on the availability of the same workshop within the year.
  • • On open slots during the next workshop.
  • • Transfers cannot be made 5 working days or less to the booked date.
  • • Rescheduling cannot be made 3 working days or less to the booked date.
  • • Rescheduling and transfers can only be made 3 times in a year.
